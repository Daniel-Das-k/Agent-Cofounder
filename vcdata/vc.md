# VC Sheet - Where Founders Find Their Investors

## Overview
VC Sheet is a platform that helps founders browse curated sheets of VC funds and investors. The platform provides organized lists of investors categorized by various criteria to help startups find the right funding partners.

## How to Use VC Sheet
Browse curated lists below, or use the left sidebar to browse the full database. See the partners at each fund and learn how to get in touch.
